About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at SIT Nagpur is committed to delivering quality education through modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented learning practices.

The department emphasizes software development, data science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and research activities that prepare students for successful careers.
