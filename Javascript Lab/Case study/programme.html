Programme Structure

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Computer Science & Engineering is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computing principles, programming, software development, data science, artificial intelligence, networking, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with laboratory practice, industry-oriented projects, and skill development.

Semester Major Subjects Credits
I Programming Fundamentals, Mathematics-I, Physics, Engineering Graphics 22
II Data Structures, Mathematics-II, Digital Electronics, Environmental Studies 22
III Object Oriented Programming, Database Management System, Operating Systems 23
IV Computer Networks, Design & Analysis of Algorithms, Software Engineering 23
V Artificial Intelligence, Web Technologies, Data Science 22
VI Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, Machine Learning 22
VII Major Project - I, Professional Electives, Internship 20
VIII Major Project - II, Seminar, Professional Ethics 18
